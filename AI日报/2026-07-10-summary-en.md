## [EU Parliament Passes Chat Control 1.0 via Procedural Tactic](https://www.patrick-breyer.de/en/eu-parliament-greenlights-chat-control-1-0-breyer-our-children-lose-out/) ⭐️ 9.0/10

On July 9, 2026, the European Parliament allowed Chat Control 1.0 to pass, permitting mass scanning of private messages without a warrant, despite a majority of voting MEPs opposing it (314 against, 276 in favor). The measure was rejected twice in March but passed because the motion to reject failed to secure the required absolute majority of 361 votes. This decision undermines digital privacy and end-to-end encryption in the EU, setting a precedent for mass surveillance of private communications. It affects millions of users on platforms like Instagram, Gmail, and Skype, and raises concerns about democratic processes and fundamental rights. The regulation allows US tech companies to scan private messages without a warrant or prior suspicion, effective until April 3, 2028. Public social media posts and cloud storage files were already scannable; the change applies to direct messages and emails.

**Background**: Chat Control 1.0, officially the Regulation to Prevent and Combat Child Sexual Abuse (CSAR), was first introduced as a temporary measure in 2021. It aims to mandate detection and reporting of child sexual abuse material, but critics argue it effectively forces mass surveillance of all private communications, undermining encryption and privacy rights. The European Parliament had rejected its extension in March 2026, but the latest vote used an urgency procedure requiring an absolute majority to block, which opponents failed to achieve.

## [OpenAI Releases GPT-5.6 with SOTA on ARC-AGI-3](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-6/) ⭐️ 9.0/10

OpenAI released GPT-5.6, a frontier model available in three sizes (Luna, Terra, Sol), achieving a new state-of-the-art score of 7.8% on the ARC-AGI-3 benchmark and introducing advanced intent understanding and original image detail preservation. This release pushes the frontier of AI reasoning and multimodal capabilities, with GPT-5.6 Sol being the first verified frontier model to beat an ARC-AGI-3 game. The improved intent understanding reduces the need for explicit step-by-step instructions, making AI interactions more natural and efficient. GPT-5.6 comes in three sizes: Luna (smallest), Terra (medium), and Sol (largest). The model preserves original image dimensions and details, and the developer guide recommends still stating important constraints explicitly despite improved intent understanding.

ARC-AGI-3 is the latest version that forbids certain harnesses and requires models to solve novel puzzles. Intent understanding refers to an AI's ability to infer a user's underlying goal without explicit step-by-step instructions.
